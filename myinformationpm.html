Tubitak

"Investors' Risk in Virtual Gaming and Cryptocurrency Platforms Taking Attitude", cryptocurrencies, especially Bitcoin and Ethereum, have recently attracted a lot of attention. The research examines the intersection points, risk-taking attitudes and investment behaviors of investors and virtual players and reveals their relationships. For this purpose, quantitative and qualitative research methods will be used. At the end of the research, recommendations are provided on the common factors and risk attitudes between cryptocurrency investors and virtual gamers.

Visual Data Processing with Data Mining

In our pandemic response project, we've created a "Mask Control" system using CNN and machine learning to detect face masks accurately. Operating in crowded places like hospitals and transport hubs, this system enhances safety. By utilizing the "Face Mask Detection Dataset," we gathered, scaled, and processed data for model training. Our project's outcome includes visualized results, demonstrating the successful integration of the system into everyday life.
